Our Story

A Name Built on
Reputation.

Chagrin Valley Cornerstone was founded in Chagrin Falls on a simple principle: every home deserves exceptional care, thoughtful craftsmanship, and lasting quality. For over a decade, we have proudly served homeowners throughout the Eastside communities, including Chagrin Falls, Bainbridge, Solon, Pepper Pike, Moreland Hills, Orange Village, and the surrounding areas. From hillside properties along the Chagrin River to historic residences near the falls, we've had the privilege of helping families build, renovate, and preserve some of the area's most distinctive homes.

Our approach is intentionally hands-on. We do not rely on rotating subcontractors — our team is present on every project, ensuring consistency, accountability, and a level of detail our clients expect. Equally important is our commitment to the people we serve. We take the time to understand each client's vision, communicate clearly throughout every phase, and deliver results that reflect both quality and care. Much of our work comes through long-standing relationships, repeat clients, and referrals — a reflection of the trust we've built over the years.

We understand that building or renovating a home is a significant investment. It should be handled with professionalism, discretion, and a genuine respect for the home and the people who live in it.

— Will, Owner & Operator, Chagrin Valley Cornerstone
